Story

The Journey

SmallBets started as a simple idea: instead of waiting for the perfect project, just start building. Every day. Every week. Compound those small experiments.

Some projects took an afternoon. Others evolved over months. A few became production systems used by real people. Many "failed" - but each failure was a lesson that made the next project better.

The result? 170+ projects spanning AI systems, educational platforms, games, productivity tools, and more. Not because quantity matters, but because shipping is the ultimate teacher.

This portfolio isn't about impressing anyone. It's proof that consistent action, small bets, and relentless curiosity compound into something meaningful.
